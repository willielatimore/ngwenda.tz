Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers

How does the credit service work?

Our credit service is simple and transparent. You pay as low as 20% deposit, then spread the remaining balance over 3 to 18 months. We offer competitive interest rates starting from 12% per annum. Approval is typically within 24 hours after document verification.

What documents do I need to apply for credit?

You'll need: Valid National ID or Passport, Proof of Residence (utility bill or bank statement), Proof of Income (last 3 months' pay slip or business bank statement), and an active mobile number for verification. All documents must be original or certified copies.

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes! We offer free doorstep delivery within Dar es Salaam and surrounding areas. Delivery is completed within 48 hours after credit approval and deposit payment. For locations outside Dar es Salaam, we can arrange delivery at an additional cost.

What warranty do you provide?

All new vehicles come with a 1-2 year manufacturer's warranty depending on the model. Additionally, we provide 3 months of free maintenance service. The warranty covers all mechanical and electrical defects under normal use conditions.

Can I repay my credit early?

Absolutely! We encourage early repayment. There are no penalties or hidden fees for paying off your credit early. You'll only pay interest for the period you actually used the credit. Contact our credit department to get your settlement amount.

Where is your showroom located?

Our main showroom is located on Aggrey Street, Dar es Salaam, Tanzania. We're open Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM. You can also view our vehicles online and schedule a test drive.

Do you have electric vehicles?

Yes! We offer both electric motorbikes and electric tuk-tuks. Our electric vehicles have a range of 80-100km per charge, require 3-4 hours charging time, and come with a portable battery. They're perfect for eco-conscious customers looking to save on fuel costs.

Do you offer fleet discounts?

Yes! We offer special discounts for bulk purchases of 5 or more vehicles. We also provide customized credit solutions for businesses and organizations. Contact our corporate sales team for a tailored quotation.
