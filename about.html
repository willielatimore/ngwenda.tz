About Ngwenda Company Limited

Empowering Tanzania through diverse business ventures since 2017

Who We Are

Building Dreams, Delivering Excellence

Ngwenda Company Limited is a diversified conglomerate based in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania. Founded with a vision to provide quality products and services across multiple sectors, we have grown into a trusted name in the community.

Our commitment to excellence, customer satisfaction, and community development drives everything we do. From mobility solutions to creative services, we are dedicated to making a positive impact.
